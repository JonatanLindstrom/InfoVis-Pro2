Does acceptance bring happiness?

As the common hippie-related phrase "peace, love and understanding" goes, this discovery process is aimed towards looking into how the understanding part has developed over the years and how it affects happiness. From the data provided by WVS (displayed in heatmaps below) it can be seen how the acceptance of others and happiness correlates.

It can be seen that the overall happiness has been stable or improved for most countries. When looking at this compared to various questions in how justifiable or acceptable certain actions or feelings are, it gives a sense for how open-minded the respondents are in general. Even though some of these actions may not be good per se (e.g. suicide) a tolerant view about it being justifiable still gives a measurement to how open-minded the responants were.

From the data I conclude that as the understanding of others improve over time, so does the happiness of all. While this correlation in general seems to be true, it may not be true when it comes to money as the "avoiding fare on public transport" is the one action which didn't get improved acceptance.
